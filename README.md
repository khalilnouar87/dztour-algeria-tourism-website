# 🇩🇿 DZTOUR – Discover the Magic of Algeria

DZTOUR is a modern, responsive tourism website designed to promote Algeria's rich cultural heritage, breathtaking landscapes, and historical landmarks. The platform allows visitors to explore all 58 wilayas, UNESCO World Heritage Sites, traditional crafts, historical circuits, and practical travel information through an elegant and interactive user experience.

---

## 🌍 About the Project

Algeria is the largest country in Africa, home to thousands of years of history, diverse cultures, and spectacular natural landscapes.

DZTOUR aims to become a complete digital guide for travelers by providing:

- Historical information
- Tourist destinations
- Cultural traditions
- Practical travel guides
- AI-powered assistance
- Modern and responsive design
